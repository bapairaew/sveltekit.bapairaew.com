An internal chatbot on [Slack](https://slack.com/) that helps marketing team to
build their marketing campaigns on [Mailchimp](https://mailchimp.com/) quickly.
The team usually spend a few hours to build one campaign but with this both they
can reduce the effort significantly to less than an hour. They only needs to
fill in the some details in
[Google Sheets](https://www.google.com/sheets/about/) and activate the
automation via chatbot that connected to Serverless backend integrated with
various AWS services, for example, [AWS Lambda](https://aws.amazon.com/lambda/),
[AWS SES](https://aws.amazon.com/ses/) and
[AWS SQS](https://aws.amazon.com/sqs/).

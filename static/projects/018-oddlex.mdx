An internal tool that aims to provide 360 view on F&B merchants. The tool
collects data from various online sources to list available merchants in certain
areas along with their related information to help sales team to qualify the
lead and make it easier for them to reach out to these merchants. The tool is
built upon Serverless architecture and various AWS services, for example,
[AWS Lambda](https://aws.amazon.com/lambda/),
[AWS SNS](https://aws.amazon.com/sns/) and
[AWS SQS](https://aws.amazon.com/sqs/).
